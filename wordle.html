How to Play Wordle

Wordle is a word guessing game.
You have six attempts to guess a five-letter word.
After each guess, the tiles will change color to show how close your guess was to the correct word.

Examples:

  • Green: The letter W is in the correct spot.
  • Yellow: The letter I is in the word but in the wrong spot.
  • Gray: The letter U is not in the word at all.

Good luck!
